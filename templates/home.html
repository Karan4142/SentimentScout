Sentiment Scout

Sentiment Scout is a tool for analyzing sentiments and categorizing comments from YouTube videos.

How to Use Sentiment Scout:

1. To get Started, first Login or Register an account.
2. Enter the URL of the YouTube video you want to analyze.
3. Click on the "Get Comments" button to retrieve comments from the video.
4. Sentiment Scout will process the comments and display the sentiment analysis results, along with visualizations.

What It Will Do For
You

Allows Comments Download

Provides Download comments option for further analysis or review.

Generate Charts

It Analyze comments to understand the overall sentiment of viewers, by Generating charts and visualizations to represent sentiment distribution.

Save History

Saves your URL history for future reference.
